Regardless of my degree of accounting, I used to be an organic vegetables delivery business owner.
As I always wanted to be a social enterpreneur, I set up a small business to deliver healthy and chemical-free produces from farms to consumers, aiming at supporting eco-friendly agriculture and people's health . Who knows, everything has changed after I took an introduction to AI course during COVID-19 pandemic. Realizing that we can tackle problems with data, I have found my calling as a data scientist and delved into data science since then.


I love creating data visualization which is easily interpretable and beautiful-designed. My other skills include data acquisition, data analyzation and machine learning. I always keep practicing and exploring data science world through doing Kaggle projects, studying more about deep learning and reading articles from Medium.
